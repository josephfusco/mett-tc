Commander’s Intent:

It’s time to take command and start analyzing the situation…

METT-TC is a mnemonic used by the United States Army to help commanders remember and prioritize what to analyze during the planning phase of any operation. This site will take you through all 6 factors of METT-TC, and discuss them in detail. This site will also provide you with an abundance of other information such as troop leading procedures, land navigation skills, and operation order structure.
